The problem

Every absence costs time you don't have.

Managing substitutes manually is one of the school's most time-consuming administrative tasks — and it happens every day.

📞
Phone calls in the morning
Principals and administrators are calling around to teachers to find someone who can cover — often in a panic at 7:30 a.m.
📋
Paper schedules and Excel
It is difficult to see who is available, who already has too many substitutes, and which lessons are under-served.
😓
Teachers who don't know
Substitutes don't get notified in time and don't know where to go, what class to take or what to do.
Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't need

Sublify is built specifically for Swedish elementary and high schools — with the workflows your administrators actually use.

📅
Visual weekly schedule
See all classes' schedules in a clear calendar view. Mark a teacher as absent and instantly see which lessons need to be covered.
⚡
Smart temporary assignment
The system automatically shows which teachers are available, respects limits on the number of substitutes per week and month, and blocks those who already have lessons.
👥
Separate permissions
Administrators manage everything. Teachers log in and see their own schedule. Clear roles — no one sees more than they need to.
🏫
Several schools
Run multiple schools' schedules in the same system. Each school's data is completely separate — no mixing, no mistakes.
💬
Built-in chat
Send messages directly to specific teachers or the entire college. Attach files. All communication about substitutes in one place.
📱
Works on all devices
Web-based — no app to install, no updates to manage. Works on computer, tablet, and phone.
How it works

From absence notification to covered lesson

Four steps. Over three minutes.

1
Teachers report absence
The administrator marks the teacher as absent in Sublify and chooses which days.
2
The system shows gaps
All lessons without a teacher are displayed directly in the substitute tab with time, class and subject.
3
Assign a substitute
Choose from the list of available teachers — the system will show you who is available and has capacity.
4
The substitute is announced
The substitute sees their assignment when they log in. Everything documented, nothing forgotten.
